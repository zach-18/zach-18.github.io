Humidity

Humidity Map

As shown by the plot, there does not appear to be a correlation between latitude and humidity. The scatter of dots across all latitudes shows no relationship. It is interesting, however, to note that the occurence of low humidity at the equator is less common than higher humidity at the equator. This is to be expected in a tropical climate.
